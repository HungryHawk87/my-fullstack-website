  • By the Buyer if there has been a material breach of any representation, warranty, or covenant of the Seller that has not been cured within ten (10) days after written notice;
  • By the Seller if there has been a material breach of any representation, warranty, or covenant of the Buyer that has not been cured within ten (10) days after written notice.
